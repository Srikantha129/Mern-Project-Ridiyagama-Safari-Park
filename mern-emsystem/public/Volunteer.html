Recruitment Windows Begin Quarterly

You can become a Conservation Ambassador and contribute to making the Ridiyagama Safari Park a lovly place to visite by signing up to volunteer. By discussing the incrediblecreatures and conservation efforts of the Ridiyagama Safari Park, you will have the chance to interact with visitors from all over the world and encourage them to become friends with the wildlife.

Your job as a volunteer will be to interact with visitors and guide them though their experience at the Ridiyagama Safari Park. Park has information ambassador stations that provide volunteers the chance to welcome visitors, assist them with their plans, motivate them to support environmental causes, and much more. To enable visitors from all over the world to buil enduring memories and promote a dedication to conservation, volunteers work to create a warm environment. You will increase your expertise in the subject of conservation, develop your customer servise skills, and support the aim to save species all over the globe as an information ambassador

We are seeking individuals who:

  • Are 18 years or older
  • Positivity and a willingness to closely follow our world-class regulations, procedures, grooming, and customer service standards are required.
  • Are passionate about conservation and saving species worldwide
  • Can commit to volunteering 60 hours per year, and are reliable and responsible.

NOTE:
Non of our volunteer options include the handling or direct interaction of animals. Teens under the age of 18 are not currently eligible to volunteer with us, but those who are interested might participate in the Youth programs of the Education Department. Additionally, kindly be aware that most of resources to our volunteer positions are long-term. We devote a significant amount of resources to our volunteer program and don't provide occasional or transient programs for civic engagement. Thank You!

Expectation of Volunteers:

  • Work independently
  • Follow uniform guidelines
  • lead by example and with kindness
  • Maintain positive interactions, both internally and externally
  • Be proactive in finding information, speaking with guests, etc.
